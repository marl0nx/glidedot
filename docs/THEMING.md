# Theming & UI Customization

> Personalize the look and feel of your glide. instance with custom branding, logos, and accent colors directly from the dashboard.

glide. offers built-in white-labeling features that allow you to customize the look and feel of your instance directly from the dashboard. You don't need to change any source code to match your company's branding!

To access the theming options, navigate to **Admin > Theming** in your glide. dashboard.

## 1. Logo Customization

You can replace the default "glide." text logo with your own branding in the top navigation bar.

- **Logo Type:** Choose between a simple **Text** logo or an **Image** logo.
- **Logo Text:** If using text, type your company name. You can toggle a small accent "dot" at the end of the text.
- **Logo URL:** If using an image, provide the URL to your full logo (e.g., your company's wordmark or horizontal logo).
- **Minimal Logo URL:** Provide a square or icon-only version of your logo. This is used when the sidebar navigation is collapsed.
- **Logo Size:** Adjust the height of your logo (in pixels) to make it fit perfectly within the navbar.

## 2. Colors & Theme Mode

Adjust the core colors to match your brand identity.

- **Primary Accent Color:** This changes the color of all primary buttons, active links, highlights, and the activity heatmap. Pick a custom HEX color (e.g., `#FF5500`) to replace the default glide. green.
- **Theme Mode:** Choose whether your glide. instance should default to **Dark Mode** (recommended) or **Light Mode**. Users cannot override this setting; it applies globally to your instance.
- **Custom Background Color:** Want a slightly different shade of dark or light for the app background? You can enter a custom HEX code (e.g., `#0F172A`) to replace the default background color across all pages.

> [!NOTE]
> All changes are saved automatically and applied globally for all users using your glide. instance.
